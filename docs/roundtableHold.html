The Roundtable Hold

Roundtable Hold is a Location in Elden Ring. The Roundtable Hold is found after Melina invites to take you there. This area can only be accessed via fast travel. It is a place where the Two Fingers gathers worthy tarnished to pursue the shardbearers and eventually repair the Elden Ring.
There are a number of NPCs located at the Roundtable, this would include:

  • Brother Corhyn
  • D, Hunter of the Dead
  • Dung Eater
  • Ensha of the Royal Remains
  • Fia, Deathbead Companion
  • Finger Reader Enia
  • Knight Diallos
  • Nepheli Loux, Warrior
  • Roderika
  • Sir Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing
  • Smithing Master Hewg
  • Sorcerer Rogier
