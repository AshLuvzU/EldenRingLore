TSV collection: ADJs in boss fight dialogue

This collection, made in Cytoscape, looks at the ADJs used in different bosses dialogue in the fights.

This frist collection focuses on all the data I pulled from all the boss fights, a lot of the words are conected to many characters at once.
Youll also notice that there are repeats of the same character in the network, this is the different scene blocks for each character.

Rennala has the most words associated with just one scene out of all the dialog I pulled. Her words are etheral sounding, as her purpose in game is to birth the player anew.

Godrick is the most talkitive in his boss fight, as he has the most words associated with him and his many scenes. His theme is more treating the player as unfit for Elden Lord and how he as an Omen will take the player down.

The next couple of collections are not as interesting data wise, they have very little words associated with them, and they have a generic theme of just calling the player undeserving of the throne.

Even though its I wasn't able to find a theme with Malikeths words, he does have the least out of everyone.
