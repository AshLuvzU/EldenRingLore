Map of Deeproot Depths

The Deeproot Depths is an enormous cavern deep below the Altus Plateau. It is the source of the Ainsel and Siofra Rivers, and the furthest downward the Erdtree roots extend. The network of Greattree roots that spread throughout the Lands Between also begins here.
